About Us

Oxbridge Farm is a small family-owned farm in southwestern America.

We are located at 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA. See the Directions for how to get here.

We are open from 9am until 5pm on weekends. Please call for weekday hours. Someone is usually here but we encourage you to call us first.

Call us at 555-555-5555 for availability and current prices of our products.

What We Grow

We offer a wide assortment of locally grown products to our customers. We have seasonally available produce, including apples, peaches, pears, as well as an selection of vegetables and decorative plants. At holiday times, we have pumpkins and gourds of all sizes for Halloween, and Christmas trees in December. We also offer high quality first-cut hay for animal feed.
